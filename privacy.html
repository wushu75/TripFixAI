01

Overview

TripFix AI ("we", "our", "the extension") is a Chrome browser extension that helps travellers automatically rebook cancelled flights. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use TripFix AI.

We are registered in the United Kingdom. By installing or using TripFix AI, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Short version: TripFix AI stores everything locally on your device using chrome.storage.local. We never sell your data, never store it on our servers, and never share it with advertisers.
02

Data We Collect

TripFix AI collects only the minimum information required to deliver its rebooking service. All data is stored locally on your device using the Chrome extension storage API.

  • Travel preferences — accessibility needs (e.g. wheelchair ramp), dietary restrictions (e.g. vegan, kosher), preferred airlines, seat preference, budget limits, and hotel proximity settings you enter in the extension's profile tab.
  • Frequent flyer numbers — loyalty programme IDs you optionally enter (e.g. Delta SkyMiles, United MileagePlus). Stored locally only.
  • Trip details — origin/destination airport codes, flight numbers, and travel dates you enter when triggering a rebook scan. Not retained after the session closes.
  • Meeting address — used transiently to calculate hotel proximity via Google Maps. Not stored after the maps query completes.
  • Extension settings — your dark/light mode preference, polling interval, and notification settings.
03

Data We Don't Collect

We want to be explicit about what we never do:

  • We do not collect, transmit, or store your name, email address, or any account information.
  • We do not read the full content of your emails — only subject lines and snippets visible in your email list are scanned for cancellation keywords.
  • We do not track your browsing history or any pages you visit outside of Gmail and Outlook mail views.
  • We do not collect payment information. Any Pro tier billing is handled entirely by Stripe on our website — the extension never touches card details.
  • We do not use cookies, pixel trackers, or advertising identifiers of any kind.
  • We do not build a profile of you or share data with data brokers.
TripFix AI is not designed for users under 18. If you are under 18, please do not use this extension.
04

API Key Storage

Pro users may optionally enter API keys for Anthropic (Claude), Amadeus, and Google Maps directly in the extension settings. These keys are:

  • Stored exclusively in chrome.storage.local on your local device.
  • Never transmitted to TripFix AI's servers.
  • Sent only to the respective third-party APIs they belong to (Anthropic, Amadeus, Google) — and only when you actively trigger a rebook scan.
  • Displayed masked (sk-ant-api03-••••) in the UI at all times.

You can delete your API keys at any time by clearing them from the Settings tab or by uninstalling the extension.

Chrome's chrome.storage.local is sandboxed per-extension. No other extension or website can read your TripFix AI data.
05

Email Detection

TripFix AI includes a content script that runs on Gmail (mail.google.com) and Outlook (outlook.live.com, outlook.office.com) to detect flight cancellation notifications.

The content script operates as follows:

  • It reads only the email list view — sender name, subject line, and the short preview snippet visible on screen. It never opens, reads, or processes full email bodies.
  • It looks for cancellation-related keywords (e.g. "flight cancelled", "schedule change") combined with airline sender patterns to confirm relevance before alerting.
  • Detected flight numbers and routes are processed locally in the browser and used only to pre-fill the rebook form.
  • No email content, metadata, or identifiers are ever sent to TripFix AI's servers.
  • Email scanning can be disabled at any time from the Settings tab.
06

Third-Party Services

When you trigger a rebook scan, TripFix AI makes requests to the following third-party APIs on your behalf. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Amadeus API — flight status and alternative flight search. Data sent: carrier code, flight number, date. Amadeus Privacy Policy ↗
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — AI reasoning to rank rebooking options. Data sent: anonymised trip details and your preferences (no name, email, or payment data). Anthropic Privacy Policy ↗
  • Google Maps API — distance calculation between hotel options and your meeting address. Data sent: hotel address and your meeting address (point-to-point only). Google Privacy Policy ↗
  • Google Fonts — Inter and JetBrains Mono typefaces loaded on the landing page. Your IP address may be logged by Google. Google Fonts Privacy ↗

We do not have affiliate relationships with any of the airlines, hotels, or booking platforms surfaced as rebooking options. We do not receive commission from bookings you make.

07

Data Retention

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you are in complete control of retention:

  • Preferences and settings persist in chrome.storage.local until you clear them from the extension UI or uninstall TripFix AI.
  • Session data (trip details, pending cancellation alerts, found rebooking options) is automatically cleared when the extension popup closes or within 30 minutes of generation.
  • Uninstalling the extension deletes all locally stored data immediately per Chrome's extension data policies.

We do not hold any of your data on our servers, so there is nothing for us to delete on your behalf.

08

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the following rights regarding personal data. As TripFix AI does not collect or store personal data on our servers, most of these rights are exercised directly through the extension:

  • Right to access — view all stored data via Chrome's DevTools: chrome.storage.local.get(null, console.log) in the extension's service worker console.
  • Right to erasure — clear all data from the Settings tab, or uninstall the extension.
  • Right to portability — export your preferences by copying from the Settings tab fields.
  • Right to object — disable email scanning or any feature from Settings at any time.

For any privacy enquiries, contact us at the address below. We aim to respond within 72 hours.

09

TripFix AI is not directed at children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used TripFix AI, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.
